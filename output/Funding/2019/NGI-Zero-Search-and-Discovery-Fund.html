About

NLnet manages the NGI0 Discovery Fund, a fund dedicated to search technologies which creates an open, trustworthy and reliable internet for all. The fund was established with financial support from the European Commission's Next Generation Internet programme, under the aegis of DG Communications Networks, Content and Technology.
